CO2 Levels

Atmospheric CO2 levels have increased by over 40% since the pre-industrial era, reaching levels not seen in millions of years. This rise is primarily due to fossil fuel combustion and deforestation.

Increased CO2 levels contribute to the greenhouse effect, leading to global warming and climate change.
